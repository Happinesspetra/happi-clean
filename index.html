You Might Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

1 What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We offer residential, office, post-construction, and deep cleaning services. We also handle move-in/move-out cleaning, window cleaning, and more based on your needs.
2 Do I need to provide the cleaning materials and equipment?
No. Our team comes fully equipped with all the necessary tools and eco-friendly cleaning supplies unless you prefer we use yours.
3 Are your cleaners trained and trustworthy?
Yes. All our staff are professionally trained, background-checked, and committed to providing high-quality, respectful service.
4 How do I book a cleaning appointment?
You can book through our website, WhatsApp link, or call us directly. We offer flexible scheduling to suit your convenience.
5 What areas do you cover?
We currently serve homes and businesses across Abuja, Nigeria, and are expanding to surrounding areas.
6 How much do your services cost?
Prices vary depending on the size of the space and the type of cleaning. Contact us for a free quote today.
7 Do you offer same-day or emergency cleaning services?
Yes, we offer same-day cleaning services based on availability. Extra charges may apply for urgent or after-hours requests.
8 Are your cleaning products safe for kids and pets?
Absolutely. We use non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for both children and pets.
9 How long does a typical cleaning take?
This depends on the size and condition of the space. For most apartments or small offices, it typically takes 2–4 hours.
10 What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?
Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy, contact us within 24 hours and we'll fix it at no extra charge
